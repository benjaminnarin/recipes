# Dark and Stormy

Based on recipe from [here](https://www.thespruceeats.com/dark-n-stormy-recipe-759637)

## Ingredients

* 2 oz Doorly's 12 Rum
* 3 oz Ginger Beer (Tested with Bundaberg)
* .5 oz Freshly Squeezed Lime Juice
* Lime Wedge for garnish 

## Instructions

1. Add Lime Juice
2. Add Ginger Beer
3. Add Rum
4. Garnish
